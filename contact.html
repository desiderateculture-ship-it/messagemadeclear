Frequently Asked Questions

Before you reach out.

How much does this cost?

Pricing depends on your specific needs and scope. We discuss this during your free Clarity Call and create a custom proposal.

How long does the process take?

Typically 4–8 weeks from Clarity Call to launch. We work at a pace that feels right for you. (The flagship 1-on-1 intensive is designed to deliver clarity and a live website in as little as 7 days.)

What if I'm not sure if I'm ready?

That's exactly what the free Clarity Call is for. We'll explore your situation, answer your questions, and you can decide if it's the right time.

Can you work with me if I'm just starting out?

Absolutely. The best time to get clear on your message is before you build your business. We'll help you position yourself from day one.

What if I've already tried other coaches or websites?

No problem. Many of my clients have worked with others before. We'll learn from what didn't work and build something that does.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. After your website launches, we can discuss ongoing support, updates, and optimization options.
